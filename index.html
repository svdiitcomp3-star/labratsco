AgroSense Dynamics is an advanced smart agricultural protection system designed to protect lands and gardens from animal disturbances using smart deterrent technologies.


About AgroSense Dynamics

AgroSense Dynamics is an innovative 3D printed robotic system that remains fixed in one location and protects crops from animals, especially peacocks and other field intruders. It integrates multiple safe deterrent technologies such as light signals, sound emitters, sprinkler activation, and reflective mechanisms to prevent crop damage in an eco-friendly way.
